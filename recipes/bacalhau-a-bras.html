Bacalhau à Brás

Description

Bacalhau à Brás is a beloved Portuguese dish combining shredded salted cod, thin fried potatoes, eggs, and onions for a creamy, savory, and satisfying flavor.

Ingredients

  • Desalted codfish (bacalhau)
  • Eggs
  • Onion and garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Thinly cut potatoes (matchstick-style)
  • Parsley and black olives
  • Salt and pepper

Preparation Steps

  1. Soak the codfish in water for 24 hours, changing the water several times, then boil and shred it.
  2. Fry the potato sticks until golden and crispy; set aside.
  3. In a pan, sauté onions and garlic in olive oil, add the shredded cod, and cook for a few minutes.
  4. Mix in the fried potatoes, then pour in beaten eggs, stirring gently until creamy but not overcooked.
  5. Garnish with chopped parsley and black olives before serving.
